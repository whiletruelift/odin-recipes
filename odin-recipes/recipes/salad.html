Fresh Garden Salad

Home

Description

A fresh, healthy salad loaded with vegetables and a light dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups lettuce
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Toss lettuce, cucumber, tomato, and onion in a bowl.
  3. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for dressing.
  4. Drizzle dressing over salad and top with croutons.
  5. Serve immediately.
