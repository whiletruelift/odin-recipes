Lasagna

Home

Description

This lasagna is a hearty, cheesy, and comforting Italian classic. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. Cook the ground beef until browned.
  3. Layer noodles, meat, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
